Data, Information & Content

All data and content will be provided by the Wauskewin Heritage Park. The content will be curated from the exhibit space as well as the archaeological research taking place within the park. In order to ensure culturally-responsible representation of First Nations cultures, approval of culturally-specific content from the elders on the board will be necessary.
